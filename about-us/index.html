About Us

Our Story

To us, beekeeping isn't a career or an industry, it's a family tradition. Beekeeping is part of our roots, tracing its origin back to a small family farm in Switzerland. Keeping bees is a tried-and-true way for even local-scale farmers to help pollinate their crops, and honey is an amazing all-natural sweetener that is as versatile as it is delicious!

Our Apiary

Based in just outside of Ottawa in Vars, our bee farm is located on our family property. The small stretch of rural land is nestled between sprawling farmers' fields, giving the bees plenty of territory to roam and gather the materials they need to make honey.
